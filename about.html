Our Mission

It is an independent bookshop, mainly filled with antique and second hand books, but has new books too. The building is an old, squat house, built in the 1600s and overlooks the pond in cromford. The bookshop is over three floors and is full of tiny rooms and crevices stacked with books. Books are everywhere, shelves cover every surface apart from a narrow strip of floor used for a walkway. Books are stacked in piles on the floor, the stairs, EVERYWHERE. They actually had to get steel posts to support the ceilings, and they were caving in from the sheer weight of books. There is no rhyme or reason to the layout, and if you have come to find a particular book, good luck with that!
